    That isn‚Äôt to say recovering from Katrina was easy by any stretch of the imagination.     These people had lost their homes and loved ones, and they had to go for stretches of time without knowing if they would have food or clean water.     The researchers who published this paper got kind of lucky. Their paper started out as a study of low-income adults enrolled in community colleges around the US.
